Incentive Distribution & Claim Rules

Distribution Time: ASTRO airdrops will be distributed within 7 working days after the mainnet launch; cross-chain incentives are distributed on the 5th of each month; Starlink exclusive benefits are unlocked immediately after review.
Claim Method: All incentives are automatically credited to the bound wallet address, no manual claim is required; the transaction record can be viewed on the official block explorer.
Unclaimed Incentives: Incentives that are not credited due to wallet address errors will be retained in the community treasury for 3 months; users can apply for reissuance with valid proof within the time limit.
Tax Rule: All ASTRO incentives are not subject to additional handling fees; the corresponding Gas fee for receiving tokens is borne by the user, and Starlink users can enjoy Gas fee subsidy.

Anti-Arbitrage & Fairness Rules

The official will use on-chain behavior analysis technology to identify and block wool mining addresses, including bulk address registration, high-frequency transfer, and cross-chain brushing behavior.
Users who are found to have arbitrage behavior will have their incentive qualification cancelled permanently, and the already received incentives will be recovered to the community treasury through smart contract.
The community DAO will vote on the adjustment of incentive rules every quarter to adapt to the ecological development and ensure the long-term fairness of the incentive system.
All anti-arbitrage judgments are based on on-chain data, and the results are open and transparent; users who have objections can appeal through the official community.
