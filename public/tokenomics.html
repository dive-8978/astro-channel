Tokenomics

ASTRO: The fuel of the intent economy.

Total Supply

Total fixed supply: 1,000,000,000 ASTRO. No additional minting, ensuring long-term scarcity and value stability.

Utility & Execution Fuel

ASTRO serves as the native gas for all intent execution, node computing, and cross-chain validation. All network fees flow back to ecosystem participants.

Deflationary Mechanism

Automatic on-chain burn from transaction fees and execution costs. Circulating supply decreases permanently over time, creating deflationary pressure.

Governance Rights

ASTRO holders control network governance, including protocol upgrades, node parameters, risk policies, and ecosystem fund allocation.

Ecosystem Incentives

Rewards for node operators, developers, liquidity providers, and early contributors to build a sustainable intent economy.
