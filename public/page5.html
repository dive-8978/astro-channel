Dual Token Core Positioning

The dual token system forms a complementary ecological closed loop, with clear division of labor and mutually reinforcing value:

MemeAstro (MA) - Ecological Cold Start & Community Token

Core function: Realize project low-cost cold start, community user accumulation, and ecological initial fund-raising. As a meme token, it has high liquidity and community attributes, and is the entry point for users to participate in the AstroChannel ecosystem.

AstroCoin (ASTRO) - Ecological Native & Value Anchor Token

Core function: Undertake ecological operation, incentive distribution, DAO governance, and ecological value anchoring. As the native token of the ecosystem, it is the core medium for all ecological activities, and its value is linked to the development of the AstroChannel ecosystem.

Dual Token Value Linkage Mechanism

MA holding is the only qualification for ASTRO airdrop; the higher the MA holding and the longer the holding time, the more ASTRO airdrops users can get.
MA trading tax is the main source of the community treasury, which is used for ASTRO airdrop, ecological incentive and product R&D, forming a capital closed loop.
MA users who complete Starlink Phone binding can get additional ASTRO incentive, and the dual token benefits are superimposed.
ASTRO DAO governance can adjust MA ecological rules (such as tax ratio, airdrop ratio), and the community jointly decides the ecological development direction.

Token Circulation Core Rules

No team manual intervention in token price; all circulation rules are written in smart contracts, on-chain and open and transparent.
ASTRO airdrop and incentive distribution are automatically calculated based on on-chain data, no manual operation, to ensure fairness.
Community treasury funds are managed by multi-signature wallet, and all usage needs to be approved by DAO voting, no single person has the right to dispose.
Regularly release token circulation data and treasury fund usage details, accepting community supervision.
