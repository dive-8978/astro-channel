Three Core Products (Ecological Closed Loop)

All products are developed around cross-chain core + AI empowerment + Starlink linkage, with lightweight launch of minimum viable functions and phased iteration of advanced features.

AstroCross - Zero-Fee Cross-Chain Aggregator: The core product of the ecosystem, supporting multi-chain zero-platform-fee cross-chain, dynamic routing automatic selection, offline transaction caching, and exclusive Starlink Phone fee exemption benefits. The first phase supports Ethereum, BSC, Polygon, Solana and other mainstream public chains.
AstroWallet - AI Wealth Manager Multi-Chain Wallet: Integrated with AstroCross, supporting one-click cross-chain, multi-chain asset unified management, AI Gas fee prediction, intelligent asset allocation (lightweight), and on-chain income monitoring. Non-custodial design, local private key storage, ensuring asset security.
AstroAI - Ecological AI Service Hub: The underlying AI support of the ecosystem, providing lightweight reinforcement learning model for AstroWallet, cross-chain transaction risk warning, ecological data statistics, and community intelligent customer service. The long-term plan is to connect brain-computer interface and interstellar AI decision-making.

Starlink Phone Exclusive Product Adaptation

Deep integration with Starlink Phone hardware, exclusive functional optimization and ecological benefits, forming a unique hardware-software ecological linkage:

Exclusive UI adaptation: Optimized mobile terminal interface for Starlink Phone's screen and system, supporting one-click access to AstroCross/AstroWallet.
Network optimization: Adapt to Starlink satellite network, reduce cross-chain transaction delay under satellite network, and support offline transaction signature caching.
Hardware binding: One Starlink Phone IMEI can only bind one wallet address, and the bound address unlocks all ecological exclusive benefits.
Fee exemption benefit: Bound Starlink Phone addresses enjoy 3% MA purchase tax exemption (capped at 5000 USD) and zero Gas fee subsidy for small-sum cross-chain transactions.

Product Launch Core Principles

Low-cost launch: Based on open source technology secondary development, reduce R&D costs, and prioritize the realization of core usable functions.

Ecological linkage: All products are closely linked with MA/ASTRO tokens, and product usage is linked with ecological incentives to improve user stickiness.
